Services

Training, consultancy & systems change

I support schools, colleges and organisations to create emotionally safe, relational and trauma-informed cultures where children, young people and adults can thrive.

I believe trauma-informed practice is not about lowering expectations or introducing "soft" approaches. It is about understanding behaviour, stress, belonging and learning through a relational, developmental and neurobiological lens, whilst building environments that are both compassionate and boundaried.

“I am particularly interested in how systems either strengthen connection and belonging, or unintentionally contribute to social thinning — where stress, exclusion and repeated negative experiences gradually reduce opportunities for trust, safety and support.

Training

Training

I deliver training that participants regularly describe as both deeply reflective and genuinely practical.

My sessions bring together neuroscience, psychology and trauma-informed practice with strategies that staff can apply in the classroom, the corridor, the pastoral meeting and beyond.

Areas of training include

  • Trauma-Informed Practice
  • Relational Approaches
  • Behaviour, Stress & Regulation
  • Belonging & Inclusion
  • Attachment & Developmental Trauma
  • Staff Wellbeing & Secondary Trauma

Consultancy

Consultancy

Real change doesn't come from a report left on a shelf.

My consultancy work is collaborative and relational — I work alongside leaders and teams to explore culture, relationships, behaviour, belonging and emotional safety across the organisation.

Consultancy support includes

  • Trauma-Informed Audits & Reviews
  • Relational Practice Development
  • Behaviour & Culture Review
  • Inclusion & Belonging Strategy
  • Leadership Reflection & Coaching
  • Policy & Strategy Development

Speaker

Systems Change

Systems Change

System change support includes

Supervision

Supervision
